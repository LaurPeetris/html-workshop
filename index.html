Donald Trump

Donald Trump is a well-known American businessman, television personality, and politician who served as the 45th president of the United States from January 2017 to January 2021. Before his presidency, he was a real estate developer and the host of the reality TV show The Apprentice. Trump ran for president as a Republican candidate in 2016, defeating Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton in an unexpected victory.
